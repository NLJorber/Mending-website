Textile Waste

Every year across the world we produce 92 million tonnes of textile waste, according to businesswaste.co.uk.It’s estimated that by 2030 we’ll create 134 million tonnes of textile waste globally, unless consumer habits shift and clothing production changes significantly.

It's not just old stuff...

Overproduction, over-procurement and stocking issues lead to unworn items being thrown away. Retailers also create clothing waste by over-ordering and poor inventory management.

Mending makes an impact

Research carried out by WRAP found that extending clothing lifespan by nine months can reduce carbon, water, and waste footprints by 20%, saving £5 billion annually.

Brains like mending

This study found that working with your hands increases "life satisfaction"! Being able to fix something that you thought was no longer useful makes you feel well clever.
